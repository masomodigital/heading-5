Learning HTML: Understanding Heading 5 (<h5>)

Introduction

Welcome to the lesson on <h5>! This heading is smaller than <h4> and is often used for additional sub-sections in your webpage content. Headings help structure your information clearly.

Explanation

The <h5> tag defines the fifth level of headings in HTML. It's used for even more detailed sections under <h4> headings. Placing text between the <h5> and </h5> tags creates a heading.

Example

Letâ€™s see an example in code:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
    <title>Subheading Level 5 Example</title>
</head>
<body>
    <h5>My Favorite Snacks</h5>
</body>
</html>

When you run this code, you'll see "My Favorite Snacks" appear as a subheading on the page.
